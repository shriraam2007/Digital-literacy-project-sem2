TASK 4-Part B –Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist


Do's	
 * Do keep your privacy settings updated to control who sees your personal info.	
 * Do use a professional profile picture if the platform is used for networking.	
 * Do fact-check information before sharing or posting it online.	
 * Do highlight your academic projects and skills to attract recruiters.	
 * Do use respectful language as that portrays your image online.	
 * Do use your college email for sign-ups on academic and professional sites.	

Don'ts
 * Don't post sensitive personal details like your home address or phone number.
 * Don't become a part of cyberbullying or online harassment.
 * Don't use unprofessional language or slang.
 * Don't overshare daily activities on social media.
 * Don't post offensive or discriminatory content.
 * Don't click on suspicious links.
